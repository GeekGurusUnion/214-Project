\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{classFacade}{Facade} class provides a simplified interface to the complex subsystems of the restaurant. 

A facade for the restaurant management system, providing a simplified interface for clients.

The \doxylink{classFacade}{Facade} class is responsible for managing the interactions between the \doxylink{classWaiter}{Waiter}, \doxylink{classRestaurantTable}{Restaurant\+Table}, and \doxylink{classMenuItem}{Menu\+Item} classes. It provides a simplified interface to the complex subsystems of the restaurant, allowing clients to interact with the restaurant without needing to understand the underlying complexity of the system.

\doxylink{classFacade}{Facade} simplifies the complex interactions within the restaurant management system by offering a high-\/level interface for clients. It manages restaurant tables, waiters, and the communication between different colleagues.
